About me

Front-end developer from Nelspruit, South Africa

Hello! I'm currently transitioning into the tech world as a frontend developer through SheCodes. My career has been diverse, spanning content creation, customer support, research, and communications, and I'm excited to bring these experiences into my new role.

In 2023, I worked as a Mid Content Specialist at UDigital, where I crafted high-quality content for websites, blogs, and social media, focusing on SEO and audience engagement. From 2019 to 2022, I provided exceptional customer service at Bolt, resolving issues and ensuring a positive experience for all customers. Before that, at JoburgTV (2018-2019), I streamlined research processes and delivered engaging content, working closely with the leadership team.

My internship at the Mpumalanga Department of Human Settlement (2017-2018) allowed me to improve internal communications and write news articles and media releases. I hold a BA in Media Studies from the University of Limpopo (2017) and have earned certifications in Social Media Marketing, SEO, Paid Media Marketing, and Basic Coding.

Now, I'm diving into frontend development with SheCodes, blending my creative background with new technical skills to create beautiful, functional web experiences. This exciting new chapter is one I'm thrilled to embark on.
